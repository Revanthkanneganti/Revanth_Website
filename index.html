About me

Smart Fusion - My Journey at the Intersection of RA, Quality, and evolvingMedTech

With a Master’s in Regulatory Affairs and a strong foundation in regulatory submissions and quality assurance, I’ve found my passion in the fusion of compliance, innovation, and patient-centered design. My journey in medtech is driven by a deep curiosity for how systems work—and how they can work better. I’ve contributed to EU MDR transitions, conducted gap analyses against 21 CFR 820 and CMDR, and created a regulatory pathway for a Class II SaMD device intended for sleep apnea management. Whether refining labeling strategies or preparing regulatory submissions, I aim to blend precision with purpose—ensuring safe, effective devices reach the people who need them most.
